💍What Actually Builds a Strong Relationship▼
Gottman's 5:1 ratio: For every 1 negative interaction, you need 5 positive ones for a relationship to feel healthy. Consistency of small gestures matters more than occasional grand ones.

Presence over presents: 30 minutes of full attention beats an expensive gift given while distracted.

Say it out loud: Partners often don't feel appreciated even when the other person feels they are — because it stays in their head.

📚How Spaced Repetition Works▼
The forgetting curve: Without review, you forget 70% of new information within 24 hours. Spaced repetition fights this by reviewing material at exactly the right moment — just before you'd forget it.

How this works: Mark cards Hard (review in 1 day), Medium (3 days), Easy (7 days). Cards you know well space further apart. Cards you struggle with come back sooner. The system does the scheduling automatically.

Best practice: Study your due cards every day — even just 10 cards. Consistency beats cramming by a factor of 5.
