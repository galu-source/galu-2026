🗣The Fastest Way to Learn a Language (Science)▼
What actually works, backed by research:

1. Spaced Repetition (SR): Review words at increasing intervals — day 1, day 3, day 7, day 14. This exploits the "spacing effect" and reduces forgetting by up to 80%. Use Anki app for this.

2. Comprehensible Input: Listen to content you understand 80–90% of. YouTube channels in Amharic with subtitles work perfectly. Your brain acquires language through exposure, not grammar study.

3. Your Strategy: Amharic weekdays (Mon–Fri), Oromifa weekends (Sat–Sun). 10 minutes daily. The key is the streak — consistency beats intensity. A 90-day streak of 10 mins outperforms 10 sessions of 90 mins.

4. First 100 words: Learn the 100 most common words in each language first. They cover 50% of everyday speech. Add them to your vocabulary bank here.

💍What Actually Builds a Strong Relationship▼
The research is clear on what matters:

1. Gottman's 5:1 Ratio: For every 1 negative interaction, you need 5 positive ones for a relationship to feel healthy. Consistency of small gestures matters more than occasional grand ones.

2. Presence over presents: Undivided attention is the most valuable thing you can give. A 30-minute walk with no phone means more than an expensive gift given while distracted.

3. Appreciation expressed, not assumed: Say it out loud. Regularly. Research shows partners often don't feel appreciated even when the other person feels they are — because it stays in their head.

4. Weekly intentional act: Schedule it. The couples who thrive aren't more romantic — they're more intentional. Log every act here. Over time, this becomes proof of who you are as a partner.
